Abstract

The Uncertain Damage Ontology (UDO) is designed to represent uncertainties in damage assessment. In its current state, UDO supports the representation of undetect damage (udo:UndetectedDamage) as well as ambigious damage (udo:AmbigiousDamage). Furthermore, fuzzy values can be represented and assigned to instances of udo:UndetectedDamage by utilizing instances of udo:UncertainValue.

The namespace for UDO terms is https://w3id.org/udo#

The suggested prefix for the UDO namespace is udo

UDO is an extension of the existing ontology DOT and therefore reuses various classes and properties from it.
